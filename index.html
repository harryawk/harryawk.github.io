Tuit Tuit

A simple tweet categorization for 6 agency in Bandung. The app collect all tweets with a specified hashtag like #bandung and categorize the tweets to the 6 agencies based on keywords that user give. It uses Knuth-Morris-Prath and Bayer-Moore algorithm for the string matching. Developed with C# and ASP .NET framework.

  • Date: April 2016
